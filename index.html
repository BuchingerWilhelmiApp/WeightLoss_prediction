Weight loss predictor

The weight loss during fasting depends on many individual factors that could not be taken into account in the model, and the calculated weight loss is only accurate to an average of 86%. This model is based on the weight loss observed in more than 1500 patients. We remind you that the weight loss indicated is also due to an important water loss during the first days, it is important to hydrate more during the fasting period. The shape of the curve is due to a decrease in the basic metabolism during the fasting period, the body goes into a 'thrifty' mode.
